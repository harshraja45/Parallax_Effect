RECORDS

Rohit Sharma has etched his name in cricket history with a remarkable list of records. He is the only player in the world to score three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs), a feat unmatched by any other cricketer. His highest ODI score of 264 runs is the most ever by an individual in the format. In T20 Internationals, he has scored five centuries, the most by any player to date.

Rohit also holds the record for hitting the most sixes in international cricket across formats. As captain, he has led Mumbai Indians to a record five IPL titles, showcasing his excellence as both a player and leader.
